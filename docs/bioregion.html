Smartcontract documentation for bioregion

Bioregions are geographically centeres locations where SEEDS Citizens may decide to base off their operations. They are covered in the SEEDS Constitution on Part 2, Section 1 **Bioregional Accounts**. Bioregions are created using the `create` action, however it only becomes active as long as enough people `join` the Bioregion. Members can also choose to `leave` the Bioregion. The founder account may call `addrole` on member accounts for specifying their roles on the Bioregions.

Actions

  • addrole / addrole Add a named `role` to a member of the Bioregion.
  • create / create Creates a new Bioregion.
  • join / join Allows people to join a Bioregion.
  • leave / leave This is called when one wants to leave a Bioregion.
  • leaverole / leaverole Allows the account owner to leave a Role.
  • removebr / removebr The founder will invoke that to remove a Bioregion, removing all members.
  • removemember / removemember The founder removes a specific Bioregion member.
  • removerole / removerole The founder removes someone from a Role.
  • reset / reset
  • setfounder / setfounder This is called by the founder to change the founder of a Bioregion.

Events

Tables

  • bioregions / bioregion_table (i64) The main Bioregions table.
  • config / config_table (i64) Access to the SEEDS config table.
  • members / members_table (i64) Bioregion members table.
  • roles / roles_table (i64) Bioregion roles table.
  • sponsors / sponsors_table (i64) Bioregions deposit sponsor table.
  • users / user_table (i64) Access to the users table.
